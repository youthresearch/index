Our team

Inspired by research. United by purpose.

Youth Research Project was founded by students who believe research shouldn’t be limited by geography, connections, or experience. We’ve each pursued research in subjects we’re passionate about, and those experiences showed us how life-changing mentorship and opportunity can be. Our shared mission is to help students everywhere discover their interests, develop meaningful research skills, and contribute to the future of science and innovation.

Hi, I’m Evelyn Lee, a junior at Aiglon College in Switzerland! I am interested in neuroscience and adolescent mental health which influenced me to conduct a literature review on long term neuro-morphological consequences when exposed to chronic stress during adolescence. This research inspired me to take a deeper dive and conduct a research proposal on sex-specific effects of combined chronic social stressors to dendritic morphology during adolescence.
