The roadmap

Six steps from a spark to a finished project

Research feels intimidating because no one shows you the whole path. Here it is — and every guide below maps back to one of these steps.

Step 01

Find your question

Turn a broad interest into one specific, answerable question you can actually investigate.

Step 02

Learn the landscape

Read what's already known about your topic so you're building on real work, not starting from zero.

Step 03

Choose a method

Decide how you'll investigate: an experiment, a public dataset, a survey, or an analysis.

Step 04

Do the work

Collect and record your data carefully and honestly. This is what makes it research.

Step 05

Write it up

Turn your findings into a clear paper, poster, or report someone else could understand.

Step 06

Share it

Enter a competition, submit to a student journal, or present. Sharing makes it an accomplishment.
